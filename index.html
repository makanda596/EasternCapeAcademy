About Us

Estern Cape Academy is a Kenyan county school located in nairobi county, kangundo road. It was founded in 20001 with a student population currently at over 2000. The learning option for students is Boarding and day schoolers. Studies are carried out through a Kenyan based Curriculum. We strive to be committed to the students we are privileged to serve, in our alumni association, our dedicated, talented department and staff.
